#Europe
- Event: The French Revolution now selects the Open Border policy

- Decision: 'The League of the Three Emperors' - changed requirement 'Great Wars Enabled' to Revolution & Counterrevolution
- Decision: Russian Army Reforms now give slightly more bonuses to the AI

- Country: Made Krakow a puppet of Austria

- Cultures: Removed Siebenburgen, Added North German as an accepted culture of Austria

#Asia
- Decision: Added a decision for the owner of Saigon to purchase Annam if another great power owns it
- Country: Guangdong, Fujian and Guangxi are no longer releasable vassals
- Pops: Adjusted some pops in Indonesia

##Japan
